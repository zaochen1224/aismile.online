Is the AI Smile Generator free to use?

Yes, our AI Smile Generator is completely free to use with no registration or payment required. You can use our service unlimited times to add natural smile effects to your photos.

What image formats are supported?

We support common image formats including JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP. File size limit is 10MB. We recommend using clear, front-facing portrait photos for best results.

Are my photos saved or stored?

No, we take your privacy seriously. Your photos are only used for AI processing and are immediately deleted after processing is complete. We don't store any personal photos on our servers.

How long does it take to process a photo?

Typically, processing a photo takes 10-30 seconds, depending on image size and network conditions. Our AI algorithms are optimized to quickly generate high-quality smile effects.

How to get the best results?

For optimal smile generation results, we recommend using clear, front-facing portrait photos with the person's face clearly visible and good lighting. Avoid using blurry, side-view, or photos with face obstructions.
